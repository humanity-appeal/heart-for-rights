Our Mission

Hope for Gaza is a global humanitarian initiative dedicated to providing immediate and long-term assistance to children affected by the ongoing crisis in Gaza.

We work with trusted local partners on the ground to ensure that aid reaches those who need it most, focusing on the most vulnerable - the children.

Our programs are designed to address both immediate needs like food, medicine, and shelter, as well as long-term recovery through education and psychological support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and donations.

How will my donation be used?

90% of your donation goes directly to aid programs in Gaza, including food, medical supplies, shelter, and education. Only 10% is used for administrative costs to ensure efficient operation of our organization.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Hope for Gaza is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the United States, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. For donors in European countries, we provide documentation for tax purposes according to local regulations.

How do you ensure aid reaches those in need?

We work exclusively with vetted local partners who have extensive experience and networks in Gaza. All distributions are documented with photos and reports, and we conduct regular audits to ensure accountability.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes, we offer monthly recurring donation options that provide sustained support for our programs. Recurring donations help us plan ahead and provide consistent aid to children in need.
