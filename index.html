About Heart for Rights

Established in 2020, we are dedicated to protecting human rights and providing humanitarian aid

Our Mission

Heart for Rights is a humanitarian organization dedicated to protecting human rights and providing emergency assistance to vulnerable populations worldwide. Since our founding in 2020, we've been at the forefront of crisis response.

We work with trusted local partners to ensure aid reaches those who need it most, focusing on children and families affected by conflict and disaster.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to basic human rights, dignity, and the opportunity to thrive. Our programs address both immediate humanitarian needs and long-term recovery through sustainable development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and donations.

How will my donation be used?

90% of your donation goes directly to aid programs in Gaza, including food, medical supplies, shelter, and education. Only 10% is used for administrative costs to ensure efficient operation of our organization.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Heart for Rights is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the United States, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. For donors in European countries, we provide documentation for tax purposes according to local regulations.

How do you ensure aid reaches those in need?

We work exclusively with vetted local partners who have extensive experience and networks in Gaza. All distributions are documented with photos and reports, and we conduct regular audits to ensure accountability.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes, we offer monthly recurring donation options that provide sustained support for our programs. Recurring donations help us plan ahead and provide consistent aid to children in need.
