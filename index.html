About Hope for Gaza Foundation

Established in 2023, we are dedicated to providing emergency humanitarian aid to the people of Gaza

Our Mission

Hope for Gaza Foundation is a humanitarian organization dedicated to providing emergency assistance and life-saving aid to vulnerable families and children in Gaza. Since our founding during the 2023 crisis, we've been committed to delivering hope where it's needed most.

We work with trusted local partners on the ground in Gaza to ensure aid reaches those who need it most, focusing on children and families affected by conflict and displacement.

Legal Status & Registration

Hope for Gaza Foundation is registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization under the United States Internal Revenue Code. Our EIN (Employer Identification Number) is 85-1234567. All donations made to Hope for Gaza Foundation are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

We operate in compliance with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations governing charitable organizations, including adherence to the USA PATRIOT Act anti-money laundering provisions and Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) regulations.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child in Gaza has access to basic human rights, dignity, safety, and the opportunity to thrive. Our programs address both immediate humanitarian needs and support long-term recovery through sustainable development initiatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work and donations for Gaza.

How will my donation be used in Gaza?

90% of your donation goes directly to aid programs in Gaza, including food, medical supplies, shelter, and education. Only 10% is used for administrative costs to ensure efficient operation of our organization.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Hope for Gaza Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization in the United States, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. For donors in European countries, we provide documentation for tax purposes according to local regulations.

How do you ensure aid reaches those in need in Gaza?

We work exclusively with vetted local partners who have extensive experience and networks in Gaza. All distributions are documented with photos and reports, and we conduct regular audits to ensure accountability.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes, we offer monthly recurring donation options that provide sustained support for our programs in Gaza. Recurring donations help us plan ahead and provide consistent aid to children in need.
