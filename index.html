About Heart for Rights

Established in 2020, we are dedicated to protecting human rights and providing humanitarian aid

Our Mission

Heart for Rights is a humanitarian organization dedicated to protecting human rights and providing emergency assistance to vulnerable populations worldwide. Since our founding in 2020, we've been at the forefront of crisis response.

We work with trusted local partners to ensure aid reaches those who need it most, focusing on children and families affected by conflict and disaster.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every person has access to basic human rights, dignity, and the opportunity to thrive. Our programs address both immediate humanitarian needs and long-term recovery through sustainable development.
